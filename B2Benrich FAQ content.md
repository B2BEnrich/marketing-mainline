# B2Benrich FAQ content

**How accurate is the data?**  
Our data is sourced from real-time web crawling, official registries, and partner networks. We use AI to verify and cross-reference multiple sources, ensuring high accuracy. Unlike static databases that decay, our 'live' search capabilities fetch the freshest data available at the moment of your request.

**What is the difference between 'Find' and 'Enrich'?**  
'Find' allows you to discover new leads or companies based on criteria (e.g., "Find software engineers in Austin"). 'Enrich' takes a known identifier (like an email or domain) and returns a complete profile with detailed attributes.

**Do you offer bulk enrichment?**  
Absolutely. Our API is designed for scale. You can process thousands of records in parallel. For extremely large batches, please contact our sales team for optimized high-volume endpoints.

**What is data enrichment?**  
Data enrichment is the process of enhancing your existing data with additional information from external sources. This includes adding verified emails, phone numbers, job titles, company information, and other valuable data points to profiles and company records.

**How does Captain Data enrich profiles?**  
Captain Data enriches profiles in real-time by cross-referencing multiple data sources. Simply provide a LinkedIn URL, email, or company domain via our API, and we return comprehensive profile data including verified contact information, work history, skills, and company details.

**What data points can I enrich?**  
You can enrich both people and company profiles. For people: name, title, email, phone, work history, education, skills, LinkedIn data. For companies: size, industry, funding, headquarters, tech stack, key decision-makers, and more.

**How accurate is the enriched data?**  
Captain Data maintains 95%+ accuracy across enrichment endpoints. For email data specifically, we verify deliverability to ensure you're reaching real inboxes. Our real-time approach means data is always current.

**How do I integrate data enrichment into my applications?**  
Captain Data provides a RESTful API that you can integrate into any application or workflow. Simply call our enrichment endpoints with your API key to enrich leads programmatically. Our comprehensive documentation includes code examples for popular programming languages.

**How does the pricing work for data enrichment?**  
Data enrichment uses a credit-based system. Basic profile enrichment costs 1 credit, with additional credits for premium data like verified emails (+1 credit) and phone numbers (+2 credits). Unused credits roll over for up to 3 months.

**What is data extraction?**  
Data extraction is the process of collecting and retrieving data from various sources like company websites, business databases, and professional directories. Captain Data provides a RESTful API that allows you to query and extract B2B people and company data at scale.

**What sources can Captain Data extract from?**  
Captain Data extracts data from business databases, company directories, professional networks, and various public sources. We provide access to 500M+ professional profiles and 50M+ companies through our API.

**How do I query data with the extraction API?**  
Use our People Search or Company Search APIs with filters like job title, industry, location, company size, and more. Define your criteria, and Captain Data returns matching profiles in real-time through our RESTful API.

**Can I extract employee lists from companies?**  
Yes\! The Find Employees endpoint retrieves all employees at a target company via our API. You can filter by role, department, or seniority to get the employee data you need for your product features.

**Is data extraction GDPR compliant?**  
Yes. Captain Data is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant. We only collect publicly available professional data and provide mechanisms for data subject requests. All data is processed in accordance with privacy regulations.

**What's the difference between extraction and enrichment?**  
Extraction is about discovering and collecting new data (querying people or companies that match criteria via our API). Enrichment is about enhancing existing data (adding more details to a known profile). Both work together \- extract data, then enrich profiles for complete information.

**What are credits and how do they work?**  
Credits are the currency used to access our API endpoints. Each API call consumes a certain number of credits depending on the complexity and data returned. The credit cost is clearly displayed for each endpoint.

**What if I exceed my usage limit?**  
If you exceed your monthly credit allocation, you can either upgrade to a higher tier for better rates or purchase additional credits at your current tier rate. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit so you can plan accordingly. There are no overage penalties.

**What's included in the support levels?**  
All plans include documentation and email support. Enterprise plan includes Slack support. 

**How is my data protected and managed?**  
Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We never sell your data and you maintain full ownership of any data you collect through our platform. Data is processed in secure EU and US data centers.
